Geometric map of Kyrgyzstan

Short explanation of Project

The Geometric Map of Kyrgyzstan Project is an innovative initiative aimed at creating a comprehensive and dynamic map system that serves as a centralized Control Points Management System for geographic information in Kyrgyzstan. This project leverages advanced geometric mapping techniques and company's built in framework technology to provide accurate, up-to-date, and interactive spatial data. The map comes with an intuitive and user-friendly interface, enabling easy navigation and access to diverse layers of information. Users, ranging from government agencies to the general public, can interact with and extract valuable insights from the map.
